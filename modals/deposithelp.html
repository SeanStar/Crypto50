How to Deposit

Crypto50 allows you to deposit 4 different crypto-currencies. These currencies are Bitcoin, Litecoin, Namecoin, and Feathercoin. If you are new to crypto-currencies, I suggest you take a look at bitcoin first. The bitcoin official website provides more than enough information for you to get started.
It can be found at http://www.bitcoin.org/.

If you already know about bitcoin and how it works, but don't know about these other crypto-currencies, you can take a look at their websites:
http://www.litecoin.org/
http://www.namecoin.org/
http://www.feathercoin.com/

After you have a good idea of how crypto-currencies work, all that is needed to play at Crypto50 is the coins themselves.

A popular exchange for purchasing the crypto-currencies we support is http://www.btc-e.com/.

You can then deposit using the addresses given for each currency, your balance will reflect the deposit automatically. You may gamble with 0 confirmations, but you can only withdraw after the deposit is completed (with the listed confirmations).
